Information

Peaceful Ride attempts to facilitate a quiet and peaceful commute for passengers on SEPTA's Regional Rail Quiet Ride cars.

Peaceful Ride allows Quiet Ride passengers to send an anonymous message to their train's conductors, telling them that someone is making too much noise in the Quiet Ride car. To send that message to your conductors now, tap "Back" and then "Send Alert."

If, after you send the alert and before the conductor arrives, the noise stops, you can tap "Cancel Alert," (which appears once you have sent an alert and disappears once the conductor resolves the noise.)

Later, "Alert Status" (text in yellow) will appear. Tap to see a message from one of the train's conductors, telling you when he or she will return to the Quiet Ride car to resolve the noise.

Once the conductor has resolved the noise, "Alert Status" will appear again, this time with green text.

Later, "Send Thanks" will appear. Tap to send thanks to your conductor, if you are pleased with the way the conductor resolved the issue.

All communication takes place through the app and is discrete and 100% confidential.

We hope that Peaceful Ride improves your commute in the quiet ride car. If you know of ways we could improve Peaceful Ride (or your SEPTA quiet ride), please email us at rr625@drexel.edu.
